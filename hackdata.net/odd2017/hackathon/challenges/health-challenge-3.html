Differences in status of public health

Type of challenge
Visualization
Description of challenge

The visualization is important to show the past as well current appearance and spread of diseases in different variables.


The challenge is to build an online visualization that compares the health status of the population residing in cities against the population residing in rural districts of our country, and the factors affecting their health.

Look for
  • Can be based on geographical regions, political divisions, rural and urban areas
  • Trend of health problem differences, for instance
    • Growth of heart-attack and diabetes in urban areas whereas people die of minor health problems like diarrhea, measles in rural areas.
    • Malnutrition and obesity
    • Other health facility
    • Affecting factor
  • Education, family size, income etc
  • Accessibility to resources like
